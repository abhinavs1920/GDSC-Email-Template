What is GDSC?

Google Developer Student Clubs are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies.

Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining GDSC IIITM, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

You can learn more about it here.

Joining the Community

As a core team member

1. If you are enthusiastic about uplifting the developer spirit within our institute.
2. If you wish to share your knowledge and skills with your peers
3. If you're interested in organising amazing events to up skill and empower developers in our institute and help them showcase their skills at a global level
4. If you're willing to spare a considerable amount of time apart from your academic and personal schedule for the club and in-turn the community.

As a community member

1. If you want to be a part of a vibrant community of developers spread across the globe.
2. If you want to learn about amazing technologies from fellow peers and Google Experts and grow as a developer.
3. If you want to share your knowledge and help your peers become better developers.
4. If you're interested in building impactful products for the community.

While we have a limited number of positions for the core team members, anyone can join DSC - IIITM as a community member at any point of time.

If you’re interested in joining GDSC - IIITM as a Core Team member, please fill the form below.
Do note that technical skills do not matter much in the Core Team selection. The only major criteria for being selected as the Core Team member is your will and your determination to learn and contribute to the community, with the community.
